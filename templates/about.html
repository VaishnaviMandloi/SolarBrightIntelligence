About Solar Eco Bright

Welcome to Solar Eco Bright, where we harness the power of the sun to illuminate your world. Our innovative solar solutions are designed to provide efficient and sustainable energy to meet the modern demands of living and working spaces.

Our Technology

At the heart of our systems lies a smart battery operation mode, which adapts to your energy needs. With modes ranging from 'Battery Saver' to 'Best Performance', our batteries ensure optimal efficiency. Utilizing latitude and longitude data, our advanced ML models predict battery charge levels based on weather patterns, ensuring you're never left in the dark.

Get Started with Solar Eco Bright

To ensure the best performance of our solar solutions, we ask our users to provide the following information:

  • Date: The current date for accurate weather prediction.
  • Mode: Your preferred battery operation mode (0 for Battery Saver, 1 for Efficient, 2 for Normal, 3 for Best Performance).
  • Latitude & Longitude: Your location coordinates for precise solar energy calculations.
  • Power Consumption Rate: The rate at which your device consumes power, to tailor our energy solutions to your needs.

Our system uses this information in conjunction with weather data and our machine learning model to predict battery charge levels, ensuring you have power when you need it most.
